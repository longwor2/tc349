Unexpected Expedition

About

Unexpected Expedition is an expolation 2.5D platforming game. The game has 3 levels where each level the player is introduced to a new ability. The story of the game is about an astonaut who has crash landed on a new planet and he needs to collect the parts of his ship to leave. He also finds the abilities that he lost in the crash and they help combat the enemies on the planet. The game spaned 8 weeks and we had a team of 5 people.



What Laura Did

On this project I took the role as 3D artist and assistant manager. I created all the 3D assests in the game and the main character animations. I also served as an assitant manager and delegated work to the 2D texture artist. For this project I animated the main character in 3DSMax. I also did all the textures for the enemies. I also set up the interactive menu and did all the audio for the game.
